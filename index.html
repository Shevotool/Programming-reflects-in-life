Programming is a skill that can be applied to many areas of life. It teaches problem-solving skills by breaking down complex problems into smaller, manageable tasks, and develops organization and attention to detail.

01

Observation

Observation is the process of paying attention to something or someone in order to gather information. This process can be done using any of our five senses like seeing, hearing, smelling, tasting, and touching.

  • A scientist may observe an experiment to collect data.
  • A parent may observe their child to understand their behavior.
  • An astronomer observing stars and galaxies through telescopes.
  • A doctor observing a patient to diagnose an illness.

Analysis
