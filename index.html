Programming is a skill that can be applied to many areas of life. It teaches problem-solving skills by breaking down complex problems into smaller, manageable tasks, and develops organization and attention to detail.

01

Observation

Observation is the process of paying attention to something or someone in order to gather information. This process can be done using any of our five senses like seeing, hearing, smelling, tasting, and touching.

  • An astronomer observing stars and galaxies through telescopes.
  • A parent may observe their child to understand their behavior.
  • A scientist may observe an experiment to collect data.
  • A doctor observing a patient to diagnose an illness.

02

Analysis

Analysis is a way of looking deeper into something, of understanding its different parts and how they relate to each other. It is a process of breaking down complex ideas, systems, or data in order to understand them more fully. It is an important tool for making sense of the world around us.

  • Market research: evaluating company's position in the market.
  • Forensic analysis: gathering evidence in crime investigation.
  • Consumer analysis: understanding consumer behavior.
  • Performance analysis: evaluating strategies in sports

03

Specification

A specification is a comprehensive and clear set of instructions or requirements that defines the characteristics, features, and functionalities of a product, service, or system. It can be in the form of a document, verbal agreement.

  • Product specification: characteristics, features and functionalities of a product.
  • Food specification: ingredients, cooking time and serving size for a recipe.
  • Software: functional and technical requirements for a software system.
  • Mechanical: performance, design and materials for mechanical device.
